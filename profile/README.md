## About KCD Texas

Kubernetes Community Days (KCD) Texas is a **community-driven event** focused on cloud-native technologies, with a particular emphasis on Kubernetes and related tools within the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) ecosystem [web:52]. KCD events provide a platform for local practitioners, developers, and enthusiasts to come together to share knowledge, discuss industry trends, and network with one another [web:52].

## Our Mission

We bring together experts, speakers, and thought leaders from the cloud-native and Kubernetes communities, offering a collaborative space to explore best practices, challenges, and innovations in the field [web:52]. By attending, participants can:

- Stay up-to-date with the latest cloud-native advancements
- Learn from real-world experiences
- Connect with the local and global Kubernetes community

## Community-Driven, CNCF-Supported

KCD Texas is organized by local Kubernetes enthusiasts and supported by the global CNCF, creating a strong connection between the local and global cloud-native communities [web:52]. Sponsorship opportunities allow companies to get involved, support the community, and increase their visibility in the cloud-native space.
